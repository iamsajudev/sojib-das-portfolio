PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to Sojib Das Privacy Policy

Sojib Das ("we," "us," "our," or "the Artist") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our official website https://sojibdas.com (the "Site"), including any services, features, music streaming, merchandise purchases, or content available through the Site. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using the Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

We respect your personal data and comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European users and relevant Bangladesh laws. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you must not use our Site.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our Site, including:

  1. Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, email address, phone number, billing address, shipping address, and payment details when you make purchases, subscribe to newsletters, or contact us.
  2. Non-Personal Information: Browser type, device information, IP address, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time and date of visits, and other analytical data.
  3. Music & Content Preferences: Information about your interactions with music, playlists, videos, and other content on the Site.
  4. User-Generated Content: Comments, messages, fan art, or other submissions you voluntarily provide.
  5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze trends, and personalize content.

2. How We Collect Your Personal Data

We collect information through the following methods:

  • Direct Interactions: When you fill out contact forms, subscribe to newsletters, purchase merchandise, register for events, or communicate with us via email.
  • Automated Technologies: As you navigate the Site, we automatically collect technical data about your device and browsing actions using cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Third-Party Sources: We may receive data from streaming platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube), social media networks (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter), or payment processors when you interact with our content or make purchases.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

For users within the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  1. Consent: You have given clear consent for us to process your data for specific purposes (e.g., newsletter subscription).
  2. Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract with you (e.g., merchandise purchase).
  3. Legal Obligation: We may need to process your data to comply with legal requirements.
  4. Legitimate Interests: We may process data for our legitimate interests (e.g., improving our website, marketing our music) provided your rights do not override those interests.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. To provide, operate, and maintain our Site and music streaming services.
  2. To process and fulfill merchandise orders, event tickets, and donations.
  3. To send you promotional emails, newsletters, updates about new releases, concerts, and exclusive content (you may opt out anytime).
  4. To respond to your inquiries, comments, or support requests.
  5. To analyze website usage, improve user experience, and personalize content recommendations.
  6. To prevent fraud, protect the security of our Site, and comply with legal obligations.
  7. To display relevant advertisements or sponsored content based on your preferences.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for site functionality (e.g., shopping cart).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Site.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and track campaign performance.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your data with:

  1. Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist in website hosting, payment processing (e.g., Stripe, PayPal), email delivery, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and customer support, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  2. Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  3. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the new owner.
  4. With Your Consent: We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include SSL encryption, firewalls, secure payment gateways, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example, transaction records may be retained for tax or legal compliance. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Your Rights (For EEA & Global Users)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Withdraw Consent: For processing based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@sojibdas.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites, streaming services (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube), social media platforms, or other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country of residence, including Bangladesh and other jurisdictions. For users in the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data during transfer.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
